About Me

Hello, my name is Caleb Robinson. I am currently attending the MIT XPRO II Full Stack Development using the MERN Stack coding bootcamp. I decided to take the course because I wanted to learn a new skill. I hope to be able to use this new skill for a possible new career path. I am currently a maintenance manager for multi-family properties in Dallas, TX. When I was a freshmen in college back in 2014 I did not take it very seriously. I also had no clue to what I really wanted to do. I was given an opportunity in the construstion field doing electrical work for many chemical plants/refineries all over the U.S. During the pandemic, like most, I was laid off. I was able to find another job before the year ended but it did not last long. After multiple weeks of job searching I found a job in Dallas, TX that ended up giving one of my best life decisions. I was originally from Baton Rouge, LA, but after accepting the job in Dallas my family and I started a new chapter in our lives. I have a wife and 4 doggos that keep me on my feet daily. When I am not working I love to go fishing and take my dogs on walks/hikes.
